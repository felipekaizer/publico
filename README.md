# Felipe Kaizer's Public Repository

**Felipe Kaizer** is a graphic designer and a professor at the Department of History and Design Aesthetics of [FAU-USP](https://www.fau.usp.br/), where he coordinates, together with Prof. Eduardo Augusto Costa, the DHMM Laboratory (Design, History, Matter, and Memory).

<felipekaizer@usp.br>

His doctoral thesis, entitled [_O Drama do Projeto_](https://sabia.pub/product/o-drama-do-projeto-uma-teoria-do-design), was first published in 2022.
